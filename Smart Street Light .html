Smart Street Light

An automatic lighting system designed to control street lights based on natural light intensity. It ensures energy efficiency, convenience, and supports sustainable urban development through intelligent automation.

Project Overview

The Smart Street Light System is an automatic lighting solution designed to control street lights depending on the natural light level. It eliminates manual switching by detecting ambient light using an LDR sensor, ensuring power savings and efficient operation in streets, campuses, and public areas.

Working Principle

The system operates using a Light Dependent Resistor (LDR) sensor that continuously monitors surrounding light intensity. When the ambient light level drops below a preset threshold (during evening or cloudy weather), the circuit automatically turns ON the street lights. When daylight increases in the morning, the lights turn OFF automatically. This smart automation minimizes energy wastage and provides maintenance-free performance.
